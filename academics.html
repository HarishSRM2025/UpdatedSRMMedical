STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES

Comprehensive facilities and support for holistic student development

Sport Club

Encourages physical fitness and teamwork through indoor and outdoor sports, regular practice sessions, tournaments, and inter-college competitions.

Dance Club

A vibrant platform for students to express creativity through various dance forms, participate in cultural events, and showcase talent at inter- and intra-college programs.

Music Club

A creative space for vocalists and instrumentalists to explore music, perform at campus events, and nurture musical skills through regular practice sessions.

YRC

The Youth Red Cross promotes humanitarian values through blood donation drives, health awareness programs, and community service initiatives.

NCC

The National Cadet Corps instills discipline, leadership, and patriotism while offering military training, adventure activities, and opportunities for national-level camps.

NSS

The National Service Scheme encourages social responsibility through community outreach, cleanliness drives, rural development, and volunteer service programs.
