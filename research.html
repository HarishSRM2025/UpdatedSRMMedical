Core Research Technologies

Medical Information Technology (MIT)

Advancement of medical information technology resulting in significant improvements in healthcare services, especially in remote health monitoring.

Wearable & Smart Health Devices

Smart devices and wearable sensors incorporating a variety of health-related sensors for diagnosis, monitoring, and follow-up care.

Stem Cell Research Facilities

Comprehensive facilities including imaging, cytogenetics, molecular biology, histopathology, and radioisotope support for advanced stem cell research.

Clinical Trial Unit & Registry

Structured research studies designed to evaluate the safety and effectiveness of new drugs, medical devices, and treatment methodologies.

Microscopy & Imaging Cores

Advanced fluorescent and dark-field microscopy facilities with high-resolution scanners supporting detailed cellular and tissue analysis.

Pharmacogenomics

Research focused on understanding how an individual’s genetic makeup influences drug response, enabling personalized medicine.

Cytogenetics Lab

Geriatric Care Unit
