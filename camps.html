BENEFICIARY DETAILS

Reach and impact of medical camps

Government School Block – Pullambadi

A total of 34 government schools were covered under the Pullambadi block, benefiting 5,562 students. The programme reached 2,997 boys and 2,565 girls, with 347 students identified and referred for corrective spectacles.

Corporate Camps

Health screening camps were conducted across 8 companies, covering 1,202 employees. Based on clinical findings, 84 individuals were referred for further medical evaluation and care.

Festival Camps

Twelve festival medical camps were organized during major religious and community events, reaching a total of 2,555 beneficiaries. Among them, 52 individuals were identified and referred for specialized medical services.

Residential Camps

Residential health camps were conducted in 8 special schools, benefiting 814 children. Of these, 177 students required advanced care and were referred to higher medical facilities.

General OPD Medical Camps

Nineteen General OPD medical camps were conducted across rural and urban centers, serving 2,053 beneficiaries. Clinical screening led to 93 referrals for further diagnosis and treatment.

DBCS & General Medical Camps

A total of 30 DBCS and General Medical Camps were organized, providing healthcare services to 2,496 individuals. Following evaluation, 107 beneficiaries were referred for specialized medical follow-up.
