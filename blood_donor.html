Introduction & History

Started: June 2009

Total no of MLT trained: 100

The Blood Bank caters to patients from Trichy and surrounding districts, ensuring quality service at affordable cost.

Objectives

  • Issue the right blood at the right time.
  • Prevention of transfusion reactions.
  • Proper disposal of expired & infected units.
  • Universal safety precautions.

Components Prepared

  • Packed Red Blood Cells (PRBC)
  • Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)
  • Platelet Concentrate
  • Cryo Precipitate

Staff Training

Officers & technicians trained at Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University — 15 days basic training + 7 days component separation.

Facilities Available

Investigations

  • Blood Grouping & Rh Typing
  • Du Test
  • Coombs Test
  • Screening – HIV, HBsAg, HCV, VDRL, MP
  • Cross Matching (Saline & Coombs)
  • Sterility checks

Procedures

  • Voluntary blood collection
  • Blood donation camps
  • Blood grouping for students & organizations

Education & Research

Academic Programme

  • Classes for MBBS & MD Students
  • Practical teaching — Grouping & Rh Typing
  • Technician training

Research

Study on Incidence of Transfusion Transmitted Diseases (ongoing).

Services Offered

  • Blood grouping & Rh typing for patients and donors
  • Tube method forward & reverse grouping
  • Du test (Rh confirmatory test)
  • Donor hemoglobin estimation
  • Coomb’s test – Direct & Indirect
  • Screening tests – HIV, HBsAg, HCV, Malaria & Syphilis (Elisa method)
  • Component separation (PRBC, FFP, PC & Cryoprecipitate)
  • Cross matching – Saline and Coomb’s phase (Tube and Gel method)
  • QC of Anti-sera, QC of PRBC, FFP & PC
  • Sterility check of empty bags & blood bags
  • Investigation of transfusion reaction
  • Conducting In-house & Outreach blood donation camps
  • In-house & Outreach blood grouping camps
