About Us

Trichy SRM Medical College Hospital and Research Centre (TSRMMCH&RC) is a unit of SRM Institute of Science and Technology Trust. The institution is located at Irungalur village of Tiruchirappalli district of Tamil Nadu. TSRMMCH&RC is recognised by National Medical Commission and is affiliated to The Tamil Nadu Dr MGR Medical University, Tamil Nadu. TSRMMCH&RC, has a vast Eco-friendly and green campus anda well-designed infra-structure for college, hospital and hostels and staff quarters. TSRMMCH&RC has full strength of faculties in all departments and has a very strong academic protocol.

Our History & Timeline

Tracing the milestones and growth of Trichy SRM Medical College Hospital and Research Centre.

SRM (Sri Ramasamy Memorial) Group ventured into higher education in 1984, marking the beginning of a journey rooted in the belief that education and service must go hand in hand. At the heart of the Trichy SRM campus is the leadership of Dr. R. Shivakumar, MD, PhD, Chairman - a leader known not just for his credentials, but for his deep commitment to learning, research, and service. A clinician, academician, and passionate researcher, he brings an energy that inspires both students and faculty. His approach to leadership is hands-on and people-centric, with a clear belief that education and healthcare must evolve continuously to truly serve society.

Under his guidance, the Trichy campus has grown into a close-knit yet diverse academic community, bringing together institutions such as Trichy SRM Medical College Hospital & Research Centre, SRM Trichy College of Nursing, SRM TRP Engineering College, SRM Institute of Hotel Management, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, and SRM Trichy Arts and Science College. Together, these institutions share a common culture - one that values curiosity, compassion, and responsibility toward the community they serve.

This journey is built on a strong foundation laid by Dr. T. R. Paarivendhar, Founder Chancellor of the SRM Group, whose vision since 1984 has been to make quality education accessible and meaningful. The establishment of the Trichy SRM Medical College Hospital & Research Centre reflects his long-standing dream of reaching the rural population of the Perambalur region with both healthcare and opportunity

TSRMMCHRC is the ideal place for nurturing the growth and development of every student to be the bright doctors of tomorrow who will strengthen health care delivery from the grass root level.Our hospital serves as a lifeline for approximately 100 surrounding villages and remote districts such as Ariyalur and Kallakurichi. These areas, often characterized by limited access to healthcare facilities and resources, rely heavily on our institution for essential medical services and expertise. By extending our reach to these underserved communities, we play a crucial role in bridging the gap in healthcare disparities, ensuring that residents have access to timely and comprehensive medical care when they need it most
